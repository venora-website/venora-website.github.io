Follow the meandering river uphill through stunning scenery, waterfalls and immersive forest glades. Count the 27 bridges that ford the river as you go. Be surprised at hidden sacred shrines and Massrock, and learn of their origins, particularly during the Famine era. Rest and relax at strategic viewpoints to the sound of flowing water. Return by forest tracks, listening to tales of the mighty warriors Cu Chulainn who roamed the hills of Cooley. A circular walk within Nature.

  • Min-Max Number: 4-15
  • Duration: 2.5 Hours
  • Fitness Level: Basic, downhill country roads and grassy paths
  • Price Per Person: €20
  • Family rates (2 adults 2 children) €60.
