Warrior Walks

Off-The-Beaten-Track walks on the Cooley Peninsula and Ring of Gullion

Walks

Gentle hill walks vie with quirky forest and river trails, awash with sacred sites and Victorian follies, bronze age settlements and famine walls. At a slower pace, biodiverse mindful walks can soothe the spirit. All walks are set among the stunning scenery of the unexplored mountains Cooley and Ring of Gullion, home of mighty warriors, Cu Chulainn and Fionn mac Cumhaill. Above all, the visitor will experience a huge welcome!

About

Based in Ravensdale, North Louth, local guide Venora will share her passion for this landscape, its mountains and forests, through its history, storytelling and nature and her intimate knowledge of this borderland area.

Learn More

Location

Ideally located on the M1 corridor between Dublin and Belfast , Venora's walks offer a great 'awayday ' for visitors. Choose a route on the stunning  low-level hills and valleys, along gorgeous rivers and waterfalls, or  winding forest trails.  All are suitable for families, friends and the culturally curious.
