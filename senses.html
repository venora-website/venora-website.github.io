A slow, immersive walking experience in nature, with lunch. Experience a gentle mindful walk and holistic forest bathing in Feede woods. Connect with the landscape, legends and history of the Flurry Valley. Forage in season. Visit a food artisan at quirky farmhouse for a vegetarian, 3 course home produced lunch in the airy conservatory. Enjoy the sensory and healing herb garden and orchard, receiving a complimentary gift from garden or larder.

  • Min-Max Number: 4-15
  • Duration: 2.5 Hours
  • Fitness Level: Basic, downhill country roads and grassy paths
  • Price Per Person: €20
  • Family rates (2 adults 2 children) €60.
